## Question 3  
### What is the Internal Locus of Control? What are the Key Points?

**Internal Locus of Control:**  
Internal Locus of Control is the belief that you are responsible for your own success and outcomes through your actions, decisions, and effort.

**Key Points:**  
- Focus only on what you can control like your efforts and attitude.  
- Avoid blaming external factors.  
- Taking responsibility increases motivation and progress.

---

## Question 4  
### Key Points to Build a Growth Mindset

- Embrace challenges  
- Learn from criticism  
- View failures as learning opportunities  
- Focus on effort over talent  
- Step outside your comfort zone  
- Practice continuous learning
